Found Around

Find and learn about the flora and fauna around Australia with Found Around.

Found Around is a game developed by Scott Blissett in woolyungah of the Dharawal people also known as Wollongong, South Coast of NSW Australia.

Join Closed Testing

  1. Have an android phone (iOS coming one day).
  2. Join the Found Around Google Group to enable access to the app. Be sure to be logged into the same email as you are in the Play Store.
  3. Download the app from the Found Around Play Store page.
  4. Test out the app and email any feedback to me, it would be greatly appreciated.
